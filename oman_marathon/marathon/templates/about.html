About Oman Marathon Day

Welcome to Oman Marathon Day, an exhilarating event that unites runners of all ages and abilities to relish the joy of marathon running in the scenic city of Muscat.

Our event showcases three main categories of runs:

Kids' RUN

Let the little ones embark on a delightful journey with our Kids' Run, a fun and interactive experience designed exclusively for children. Filled with laughter and excitement, it's the perfect introduction to the world of running for our youngest participants.

5KM RUN

Lace up your running shoes for the 5KM Run! This moderate-distance run is tailored for both beginners and intermediate runners. Enjoy the scenic beauty of Muscat as you make your way through the course, setting personal milestones and creating memories along the way.

10KM RUN

Ready for a challenge? The 10KM Run is designed for experienced and competitive runners. Test your endurance and push your limits as you navigate the course. It's a thrilling experience that rewards determination and showcases the spirit of true marathon running.

Join us in this extraordinary marathon experience, where each run offers a unique adventure. Whether you're running for fitness, competition, or the sheer joy of it, Oman Marathon Day has something special for everyone.
