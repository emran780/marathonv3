Past Events

2022 - Remarkable Turnout

Held on February 11-12, 2022, with over 4,000 runners from over 60 countries.

The event featured a marathon, half marathon, 10K, 5K fun run, and kids' run.

2019 - Record Participation

Over 8,000 runners from 103 countries participated in 2019.

The marathon, half marathon, and 10K routes were all certified by the world-running organization AIMS.

The 5KM Fun Run saw thousands of runners raise money for good causes, and the Kids' Run sold out in record time.

2018 - Guinness World Record

Held in January 2018 with over 4,000 runners from 70 countries.

The event set a Guinness World Record for the fastest half-marathon pushing a double buggy.

2017 - Renaming of the Event

The 2017 Al Mouj Muscat Marathon was renamed the Al Mouj Muscat Marathon.

The event had over 2,000 runners from 60 countries and was held in February.

2016 - Growing Participation

The 2016 Al Mouj Muscat Marathon had over 1,000 runners from 50 countries.

2015 - International Flavor

The 2015 Al Mouj Muscat Marathon had over 700 runners from 40 countries.

2014 - Growing Participation

The 2014 Al Mouj Muscat Marathon had over 500 runners from 30 countries.

2013 - Expanding Reach

The 2013 Al Mouj Muscat Marathon had over 300 runners from 20 countries.

2012 - Inaugural Event

The inaugural Al Mouj Muscat Marathon was held in 2012 with 135 competitors.

The event was organized by Muscat Road Runners.
